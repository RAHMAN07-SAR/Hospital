Meet Dr. Batul Kotawala (BPT)

I am a qualified physiotherapist with a hands-on approach to patient care, currently completing an internship and practicing across orthopaedic, neurological, gynecological, and sports rehabilitation departments. I emphasize evidence-based practice, patient education and progressive functional recovery.

  • Qualification: BPT
  • Experience: 3 years (multi-department)
  • Specialties: Ortho, Neuro, Postpartum, Sports Injury
  • Equipment: Ultrasound, IFT, TENS, Russian current
  • Sessions: 45–60 minutes; flexible scheduling

My approach combines manual therapy, targeted exercise prescription, electrotherapy where indicated, and patient education — all tailored to your goals. I measure progress objectively and adjust plans to ensure meaningful recovery.
