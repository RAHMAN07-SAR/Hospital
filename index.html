Case Studies

Post-ACL Reconstruction — 12-week recovery plan

Initial goals: pain control, soft tissue mobility, regain terminal extension and begin progressive strengthening. By week 12 the patient returned to jogging with a graded plan.

Interventions: early mobilisation, electrical stimulation for quadriceps activation, progressive closed-chain strengthening, proprioception & neuromuscular training.

Stroke Rehabilitation — neuro recovery program

A 6-month program focusing on balance, gait training, functional transfers and caregiver education resulted in improved independence for daily activities.

Interventions: task-specific training, balance retraining, constraint-induced movement practice, and adaptive strategies.
